The Art of Harvesting Tomatoes

Introduction

Harvesting tomatoes at the right time is crucial for optimal flavor and texture. This guide will help you understand when and how to pick your tomatoes for the best results.

When to Harvest

  • Look for a deep, uniform color
  • Gently squeeze the tomato - it should be firm with slight give
  • Check if the tomato easily comes off the vine with a gentle twist

How to Harvest

  1. Gently grasp the ripe tomato
  2. Twist the tomato until it snaps off the vine
  3. If it doesn't come off easily, use clean scissors to cut the stem
  4. Place harvested tomatoes in a basket or container

After Harvest Care

Store tomatoes at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. Avoid refrigerating unless they're overripe, as cold temperatures can affect flavor and texture.
